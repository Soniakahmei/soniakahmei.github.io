Sonia Kahmei

Ph.D. Candidate in Economics · IIT Guwahati

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at IIT Guwahati under the supervision of Prof. Mrinal Kanti Dutta. My research examines Energy Access and its Determinants in Manipur. My broader research interests lie between Development and Environmental Economics. I hold an M.A. in Applied Economics from CHRIST University, Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.
